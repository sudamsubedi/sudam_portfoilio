About the Artist

📷

Sudam Subedi

Sudam Subedi is a government employee at the Federal Parliament Secretariat of Nepal. He was among the successful candidates in the recent Federal Parliament Secretariat Employees Trade Union election, receiving 116 votes as part of the Subedi-led panel.

Role Highlight: Part of a winning panel in the Secretariat’s union election, indicating involvement in staff representation and union activities.
